About Us

Discover our mission, vision, and the passionate team driving change in Kisumu's water and sanitation landscape.

Our Mission

To empower youth to take action on water, sanitation, and hygiene issues in their communities.

Our Vision

A world where every community has access to clean water and sanitation, led by empowered youth leaders.

Our Core Values

Our core values guide our actions and define our approach to achieving our mission.

Integrity

We uphold the highest standards of integrity in all our actions.

Collaboration

We work together, across boundaries, to meet the needs of our communities.

Innovation

We strive for innovation to improve the lives of those we serve.

Our Thematic Areas

Our work focuses on these three key thematic areas to create sustainable impact.

Integrated water resource management

Promoting effective policies and management systems for sustainable water resources and services.

Learn more

Waste management

Ensuring accessible and proper waste disposal, sanitation, and hygiene services for all communities.

Learn more

Climate change and environmental protection.

Promoting sustainable practices and policies to protect the environment and address climate change.

Learn more
