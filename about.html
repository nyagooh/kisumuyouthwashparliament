About Us

Discover our mission, vision, and the passionate team driving change in Kisumu's water and sanitation landscape.

Our Mission

To empower youth to take action on water, sanitation, and hygiene issues in their communities.

Our Vision

A world where every community has access to clean water and sanitation, led by empowered youth leaders.

Our Core Values

Our core values guide our actions and define our approach to achieving our mission.

Integrity

We uphold the highest standards of integrity in all our actions.

Collaboration

We work together, across boundaries, to meet the needs of our communities.

Innovation

We strive for innovation to improve the lives of those we serve.

Our Programs

Discover how we're making a difference through our various initiatives and programs.

Waste Management

Installing water points and ensuring sustainable access to clean water in communities.

Water and sanitation

Building and maintaining proper sanitation facilities in schools and communities.

WASH Education

Providing comprehensive education on water, sanitation, and hygiene practices.

Youth Leadership

Training young people to become WASH leaders in their communities.

Community Outreach

Engaging with communities to understand and address their WASH needs.

WASH Advocacy

Advocating for better WASH policies and infrastructure at local and national levels.
