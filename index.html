Our Mission

The Kisumu Youth WASH Parliament is dedicated to empowering young people to take leadership roles in addressing water, sanitation, and hygiene challenges in their communities.

Advocating for clean water access in underserved areas

Promoting sustainable sanitation practices

Training youth in WASH leadership

Building partnerships for community development

Join Our Mission

Our Programs

Discover how we're making a difference through our various initiatives and programs.

Clean Water Access

Installing water points and ensuring sustainable access to clean water in communities.

Sanitation Solutions

Building and maintaining proper sanitation facilities in schools and communities.

WASH Education

Providing comprehensive education on water, sanitation, and hygiene practices.

Youth Leadership

Training young people to become WASH leaders in their communities.

Community Outreach

Engaging with communities to understand and address their WASH needs.

WASH Advocacy

Advocating for better WASH policies and infrastructure at local and national levels.
